Who we are — Our Vibe

San Juan based Seis D' Nos has been in the local music scene since 2011.
Seis De Nos bring to the scene a cool and fresh air with some Classics of the World Music in our own way. Simple, Ease to Listen and with charm, that's our vibe!!!

A Young lady in her finest hour

Serving as background for lead singer Nicole Bures, the band has centered in performing a vibrant array of Brazilian classics, Jazz standards, and a few surprising pop adaptations.
